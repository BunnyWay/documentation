**Key features**

- **Automatic Transcription** - Automatically generates subtitles and captions for videos, eliminating the need for manual transcription efforts.
- **Transcription Translation** - Automatically translate your generated subtitles and captions into 56 languages.
- **Smart title / description / chapters & moments** - Generate and automate these video details and features for faster setup and keep viewers engaged.

## Language detection

During automatic transcription, the language of the video is detected based on the first 30 seconds of audio. If the first 30 seconds of your video do not contain any speech (for example, if they only contain music or silence), the automatic language detection may not work correctly.

<Note>
  If your video does not have speech in the first 30 seconds, it is recommended to manually set the transcription language when requesting a transcription.
</Note>
