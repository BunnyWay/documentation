<Update label="2026-03-20" tags={["Improvement"]}>
  ## Transcription language detection

  Bunny Stream now automatically detects the spoken language in your videos based on the first 30 seconds of audio, making transcription setup even easier.

  [Learn more](/stream/transcribing)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-03-18" tags={["New"]}>
  ## Vimeo2Bunny migration tool

  A new CLI tool makes it easy to migrate your video library from Vimeo to Bunny Stream. Videos are transferred directly without downloading to your local machine.

  [Learn more](/stream/vimeo2bunny)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-03-16" tags={["New"]}>
  ## Webhook signature validation

  You can now verify the authenticity of incoming Bunny Stream webhook requests using HMAC-SHA256 signature validation, ensuring your webhook payloads haven't been tampered with.

  [Learn more](/stream/webhooks)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-03-12" tags={["New"]}>
  ## Player 2.0

  The new Bunny Stream player is here with a modern interface, improved performance, and enhanced customization options. Upgrade your existing video libraries by toggling off the legacy player in Player Settings.

  [Learn more](/stream/player)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-02-11" tags={["New"]}>
  ## Mobile SDKs

  Native Mobile SDKs are now available for iOS (Swift) and Android (Kotlin), featuring a full video player, TUS-based resumable uploads, camera recording support, and token authentication.

  [Learn more](/stream/mobile-sdk)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-01-23" tags={["New"]}>
  ## Smart chapters

  Bunny Stream can now use AI to automatically generate chapters and moments from your video's caption track, helping viewers navigate your content more easily.

  [Learn more](/stream/smart-chapters)
</Update>
