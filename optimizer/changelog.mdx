  ## HEIC, HEIF, and AVIF status update

HEIC, HEIF, and AVIF formats have been promoted from beta to Public Preview, indicating increased stability and readiness for broader use. [Learn more](/optimizer/dynamic-images/formats)

<Update label="2026-04-30" tags={["Improvement"]}>
  ## AVIF output support

The Optimizer now supports AVIF as an output format, offering better compression than WebP with excellent quality. [Learn more](/optimizer/dynamic-images/formats)

</Update>
