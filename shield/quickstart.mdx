**Bunny Shield** filters malicious traffic from your website and records detailed logs for analysis. This quickstart covers setting up a Shield Zone and reviewing the Web Application Firewall (WAF) settings.

Before you begin, you'll need an existing [Pull Zone](/cdn/quickstart) to protect.

## Creating a Shield Zone

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get started">
    Click on the **Get Started** button.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/025b6da75c4b14d2da72387fc826a5773bb56130fd757240ecf945afad12ee75-image.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a WAF Profile">
    Select a WAF Profile and click **Next Step**. Using a WAF Profile reduces the potential for false positives for your web application.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/cfaf5d261dd95d5b9f2abf393e952cfbf4d86fa290038af9f39c83e754d85f5d-image.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm your plan">
    Pick a plan that fits your needs, then click **Confirm**.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/docs/c48c7fc114cbe8507c8b133bb854d8768ca797463aa3b5400d20027b50c28d98-image.png)
    </Frame>

  </Step>
</Steps>
