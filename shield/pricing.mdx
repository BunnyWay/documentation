Bunny Shield plans are tiered, so even the lowest tier delivers resilient protection. Higher tiers add more granular control, larger rule sets, and dedicated support to match tailored security demands. View current pricing at [bunny.net/pricing](https://bunny.net/pricing).

All tiers receive the same continually updated set of core security features. When enabling Shield or comparing plans, you'll see this table alongside the Pull Zone's current tier. Every Pull Zone starts in Learning mode the first time Shield is enabled.

## Basic

A solid foundation of Shield protection, including a baseline WAF package, full DDoS protection, limited inspection features, and custom access control list definitions such as IP and ASN detection.

## Advanced

Recommended for small-scale projects and commerce sites. Advanced includes a larger suite of WAF rule triggers along with [custom WAF rule](/shield/custom-waf-rule) integration so you can shape the WAF to match your application. Bot protection helps mitigate unwanted scraping and automation and can be fine-tuned, alongside expanded access list restrictions and other features.

## Business

Recommended for larger-scale projects. The Shield features and engine are more granular and better at identifying bad actors. Business comes with a wealth of additional custom rules and a large set of access control list customization options.

## Enterprise

Enterprise unlocks unlimited rules and more complex scanning, plus a dedicated onboarding session with our security engineers. Our team can walk you through the product, review your triggers and rules, recommend mitigations, and more. Enterprise also includes enhanced SLAs for Shield-related issues.
