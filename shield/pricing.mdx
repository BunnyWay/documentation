Bunny Shield is sold in four tiers. Every tier ships with the same core WAF, DDoS, and access list protections. Higher tiers raise rule caps, unlock finer-grained controls, and add direct access to our security engineers. View current pricing at [bunny.net/pricing](https://bunny.net/pricing).

<SalesCta />

When you enable Shield or compare plans, the dashboard shows a comparison table next to the Pull Zone's current tier. Every Pull Zone starts in Learning mode the first time Shield is enabled.

## Basic

Includes the baseline WAF package, full DDoS protection, basic inspection features, and custom access control list definitions like IP and ASN-based rules.

## Advanced

A fit for small-scale projects and commerce sites. Advanced includes a larger WAF rule set plus [custom WAF rule](/shield/custom-waf-rule) integration so you can shape the WAF to match your application. Bot protection can be fine-tuned to control unwanted scraping and automation, and access list restrictions are expanded.

## Business

A fit for larger-scale projects. The Shield engine runs more granular checks and identifies bad actors more aggressively. You also get a much higher cap on custom rules and access list configurations.

## Enterprise

Removes the rule cap, unlocks the most complex scanning, and includes a dedicated onboarding session with our security engineers. They'll walk you through the product, review your triggers and rules, and recommend mitigations. Enhanced SLAs apply to Shield-related incidents.
