<Update label="2026-03-24" tags={["Improvement"]}>
  ## Persistent Volumes are now generally available

  Persistent Volumes have moved out of preview and are now generally available with updated throughput limits and improved documentation. [Learn more](/magic-containers/persistent-volumes)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-03-10" tags={["New"]}>
  ## Templates

  Deploy pre-built application templates with just a few clicks. Each template includes a ready-to-run container image, and some include a sidecar database. [Learn more](/magic-containers/templates)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-03-10" tags={["New"]}>
  ## Quick Deploy

  A streamlined deployment flow that lets you go from zero to deployed in seconds. Everything from picking your image to setting environment variables is presented in a single form. [Learn more](/magic-containers/quick-deploy)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-02-09" tags={["New"]}>
  ## Graceful Shutdown

  Magic Containers now supports graceful shutdown with SIGTERM signals and configurable grace periods, giving your applications time to clean up resources and complete in-flight requests during rolling updates and scaling events. [Learn more](/magic-containers/graceful-shutdown)
</Update>
