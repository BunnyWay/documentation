<Update label="2026-03-10" tags={["New"]}>
  ## Quick Deploy

  Deploy containerized applications globally in seconds with the new Quick Deploy flow. Select an image, configure endpoints and environment variables, and deploy all from a single screen.

  [Learn more](/magic-containers/quick-deploy)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-03-10" tags={["New"]}>
  ## Application templates

  Pre-built templates are now available for popular frameworks and applications including Django, Flask, Next.js, Rails, WordPress, n8n, and more. Deploy with just a few clicks.

  [Learn more](/magic-containers/templates)
</Update>

<Update label="2026-02-09" tags={["New"]}>
  ## Graceful shutdown

  Magic Containers now supports graceful shutdown with configurable grace periods. Your containers receive a SIGTERM signal before being stopped, giving your application time to clean up connections and finish in-progress work.

  [Learn more](/magic-containers/graceful-shutdown)
</Update>
