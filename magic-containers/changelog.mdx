<Update label="March 16, 2026" tags={["New"]}>
## WordPress deployment guide

A comprehensive guide is now available for deploying WordPress with MariaDB on Magic Containers, including one-click template deployment, multi-container setup, persistent volumes, and File Browser sidecar configuration.

[Learn more](/magic-containers/guides/wordpress)
</Update>

<Update label="March 10, 2026" tags={["New"]}>
## Templates gallery

Magic Containers now offers pre-built application templates for one-click deployment, including Django, FastAPI, Flask, Go, Hono, Laravel, Next.js, Rails, WordPress, Minecraft, n8n, Umami, and more. Templates also support Eject to GitHub for full customization.

[Learn more](/magic-containers/templates)
</Update>

<Update label="March 10, 2026" tags={["New"]}>
## Quick Deploy

Quick Deploy is a streamlined single-form deployment flow that auto-detects ports, pre-fills environment variables, and gets your app deployed in seconds.

[Learn more](/magic-containers/quick-deploy)
</Update>

<Update label="March 10, 2026" tags={["Improvement"]}>
## Persistent volume pricing

The pricing page now includes persistent volume pricing details ($0.10/GB/month, billed on allocated size).

[Learn more](/magic-containers/pricing)
</Update>

<Update label="February 25, 2026" tags={["Improvement"]}>
## Persistent volume node unavailability

Documentation now covers persistent volume behavior during node unavailability, including volume retention, automatic resumption on node recovery, and recovery options.

[Learn more](/magic-containers/persistent-volumes)
</Update>

<Update label="February 19, 2026" tags={["New"]}>
## Database deployment guides

New guides for deploying PostgreSQL, MariaDB, Redis, and ClickHouse on Magic Containers with persistent volumes and multi-container configurations.

[Learn more](/magic-containers/guides/postgresql)
</Update>

<Update label="February 9, 2026" tags={["New"]}>
## Graceful shutdown

Magic Containers now supports configurable graceful shutdown with SIGTERM/SIGKILL lifecycle management. New apps get a 30-second grace period by default.

[Learn more](/magic-containers/graceful-shutdown)
</Update>
