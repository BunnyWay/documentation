Magic Containers is an edge compute platform for deploying containerized applications globally. The platform automatically identifies user locations and deploys your applications close to them, with workloads scaling dynamically based on demand.

## Key features

- **Speed that flies** - Deploy on ultra-fast CPUs and NVMe storage across 40+ regions. Milliseconds matter.
- **Security, locked down** - No shared memory. No noisy neighbors. Your app runs fully isolated and fully virtualized in its own dedicated space.
- **Scalability on autopilot** - Traffic spikes? No problem. AI scales your app instantly—within regions and across the globe. No limits.
- **Simplicity, borderline ridiculous** - Just select your image, hit deploy, and let the magic happen.
- **Everything built in** - CDN, anycast, load balancing, auto-scaling, seamlessly integrated.
