Magic Containers serves a wide array of use cases across various sectors. Below, you can explore detailed use cases to understand how Magic Containers can specifically benefit your business. Each use case provides insights into how our edge compute solutions can be tailored to meet the unique challenges and opportunities within your industry.

# [Gaming as a service](/docs/gaming-as-a-service)

In the rapidly evolving world of online multiplayer gaming, Magic Containers offers a revolutionary solution for backend infrastructure. By deploying backend applications close to gamers, it minimizes latency and enhances the gaming experience with scalable, efficient resource utilization and seamless updates.

# [AI training](/docs/ai-training-and-inference-with-magic-containers)

Magic Containers optimizes AI training processes by deploying AI workloads close to data sources, reducing data transfer costs, and utilizing edge computing to enhance responsiveness and efficiency. The platform ensures that AI models are trained with optimal resource utilization and proximity to end users, providing real-time responsiveness in applications such as object detection.

# [Image optimization](/docs/image-optimizer)

For digital content delivery, Magic Containers enables the deployment of image optimization algorithms at the edge, significantly reducing latency and dynamically scaling with user traffic. This ensures that images are delivered efficiently and adjusted in real-time, enhancing the user experience on e-commerce sites, media platforms, and more.
