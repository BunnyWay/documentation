# Deploy anywhere, anytime

Magic Containers grants you access to a realm of flexibility. ﻿Containerized applications can be deployed **wherever** and **whenever** you need them. Our platform goes the extra mile by identifying your users' locations and orchestrating the deployment of your applications near them. It ensures that your workloads are ready to appear and disappear dynamically across the edge cluster – giving you the flexibility to adapt to changing circumstances. The platform will identify where your users are and deploy the application close to them.

# AI-powered provisioning and autoscaling

Magic Containers harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to make provisioning and autoscaling a breeze. Say goodbye to manual resource management – our AI provisioner intelligently scales your infrastructure based on real-time demand, ensuring you're always running at peak efficiency.

# Self-healing functionalities

Magic Containers introduces self-healing functionalities to enhance your applications’s robustness. If your application is deployed on a specific node and the node encounters issues or fails, Magic Containers seamlessly relocates the app to another node within that region, ensuring continuous availability.

# Automatic container restart

In the event your application runs out of memory, Magic Containers takes proactive measures. The platform detects when an app is struggling and automatically attempts to resolve the issue by restarting the container, ensuring optimal performance and stability.

# Optimal performance and cost savings

Experience unbeatable performance and significant cost savings with Magic Containers. Our platform is fine-tuned for top-notch efficiency and is designed to make the most out of your resources. Enjoy cost-effective edge compute without compromising on speed and performance.

# Shared and reserved runtimes

Magic Containers offers the versatility of shared and reserved runtimes. Choose the best runtime environment for your specific needs, whether sharing resources efficiently or reserving them for mission-critical workloads. Our platform gives you the control to tailor your edge computing to your exact requirements.
