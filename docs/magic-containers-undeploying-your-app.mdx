---
title: "Undeploying your app"
description: "To undeploy your app, follow the steps below:"
---

1. Select **Magic Containers** and select your app.
2. Click on the hamburger menu icon (three horizontal dots) on the right side of the screen and click the **Undeploy** button.

![](/images/docs/65f6d32b7064846ba892b90440e4d9d0ceed334c1fea8ace0f39a92cceb8c161-image.png)

3. In the pop-up window, click the **Undeploy** button to confirm.

![](/images/docs/4fb6496c6704494ab7ed8ccebfc931cf57057c823b0e7b75421b2c0cd4abd06b-image.png)
