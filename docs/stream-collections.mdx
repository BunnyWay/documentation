Collections are an integral feature of Bunny Stream designed to help you organize and manage your video assets efficiently. Instead of storing videos in a single, unstructured list, you can group related videos into logical categories called Collections. By grouping videos into Collections, you can simplify your management workflows, apply common settings uniformly, and quickly find and share related content.

# What you'll need

Before you dive in, make sure you have the following prerequisites in place:

* A [bunny.net](https://bunny.net/) account ( [Log in](https://dash.bunny.net/auth/login?pk_buttonlocation=menu) or sign up for a [free trial](https://dash.bunny.net/auth/register)).
* A Video Library already created.
* A video file you want to add to the collection.

# Adding a collection

Adding a collection is simple. You can follow the steps below:

1. Log in to your bunny net account.
2. Go to **Stream** and select the video library

![](/images/docs/5d466d3d802e11cf8a6c8fd2cd5fe994e6e70d48abd289ca49b43baf4360de36-image.png)

3. Select the **Manage Library** tab and click on **Add Collection**.

![](/images/docs/dc313784737deb7ff625af17f970964d67555ebad361af44a5e6afca7c67cfc3-image.png)

4. Enter the name of the collection and click **Create**.

![](/images/docs/ee411636bd510e353936efb1df0f6444f8a6ae83a80fb33973c6f616296426bf-image.png)

5. You're done!

# Uploading a video to a collection

When you have your Collection in place, you can easily upload your video to a Collection by following the steps below:

1. Open the newly created collection and click on **Upload a Video** .

![](/images/docs/c77fdab8a500b76c036bc76bfc58965d3d6d3b6e6b3564092effd84db707421e-image.png)

2. In the pop-up window you can easily drag and drop or browse for the video file.

![](/images/docs/4eec82f6545d235483df905cb2ead46cd35179d1da8ed5aa55e05436699396e3-image.png)

# Moving videos between collections

If you need to reorganize your videos, you can easily move them from one Collection to another.

1. Click on the video’s hamburger menu icon and select **Move to collection**.

![](/images/docs/af9477561c6211a76f9fce14e190cee1b3cdf6e5e83253462d2ed51be4548613-image.png)

2. Select the collection from the dropdown menu and click **Move**.

![](/images/docs/d3dad86da4cfbb63c9c3c403ce27661a20a95cbacba4582be6954b8058b6abda-image.png)

3. You are done!
